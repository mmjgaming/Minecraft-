Minecraft Mods

Sodium

High-performance rendering engine that greatly improves FPS and reduces lag in Minecraft.

Sodium Extra

Adds additional graphics and performance settings to Sodium for better customization.

Iris Shaders

Shader loader that works with Sodium allowing beautiful shaders with high performance.

Inventory HUD+

Shows armor durability, inventory status, and useful HUD information on your screen.

LambDynamicLights

Makes light sources glow dynamically when held or dropped, improving immersion.

Leaves Be Gone

Leaves decay instantly after cutting trees which makes wood farming faster.

MaLiLib

Library mod required by many technical Minecraft mods like MiniHUD and Tweakeroo.

Mod Menu

Adds a mods settings menu to Minecraft where you can configure installed mods easily.

Mouse Tweaks

Improves inventory management by allowing fast item movement with mouse dragging.

No Cooldowns

Removes or reduces item cooldowns making gameplay faster and smoother.

PuzzlesLib

Library mod required for some modern Fabric mods.

ShulkerBox Tooltip

Allows players to preview shulker box contents without opening them.

Tweakeroo

Advanced utility mod for building, redstone work, and technical gameplay.

Xaero's Minimap

Highly customizable minimap that shows terrain, mobs, and player locations.

AppleSkin

Shows hunger and saturation information in the food bar.

Architectury API

Cross-platform modding API used by many Fabric and Forge mods.

Chat Heads

Displays player heads next to their chat messages for better chat experience.

Chat Animation

Adds smooth animations when new chat messages appear.

Cull Leaves

Improves performance by hiding unnecessary leaf rendering.

Fast Chest

Optimizes chest rendering to reduce lag in storage-heavy areas.
